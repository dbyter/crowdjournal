# INSPIRATION.md

Open research questions aligned to the domain directories in this repository.
Each question is phrased as a prompt that could seed a research project.

---

# bioinformatics

- Can we predict protein folding dynamics (not just final structure) from sequence alone?
- Can regulatory DNA elements be predicted with high accuracy purely from genome sequence?
- How can we infer causal gene regulatory networks from noisy transcriptomic data?
- Can we reconstruct the evolutionary history of microbial communities from metagenomic samples?
- What computational methods best detect structural variation in genomes?
- Can we infer epigenetic states across cell types without direct measurement?
- How can we predict phenotype directly from genotype at scale?

---

# biology

- Why did multicellular life evolve independently multiple times?
- What are the minimal requirements for life to emerge from non-living chemistry?
- How deterministic is evolution under similar environmental conditions?
- Why do some organisms exhibit negligible senescence?
- What mechanisms determine organismal lifespan across species?
- What drives the scaling laws observed in biological systems (metabolism vs size)?
- Why does sexual reproduction dominate over asexual reproduction in complex organisms?
- What governs the emergence of biological complexity?

---

# chemistry

- Can we reliably predict reaction pathways for arbitrary organic molecules?
- How can we design catalysts with near-perfect selectivity?
- Can we simulate chemical reactions with full quantum accuracy for large molecules?
- What governs the emergence of chirality in biological molecules?
- Can new stable elements exist beyond the current periodic table?
- How can we efficiently capture and convert atmospheric CO₂ at scale?
- What unexplored reaction mechanisms exist in complex organic synthesis?

---

# computer-science

- Does P equal NP?
- Can we design algorithms that learn optimal strategies with provable guarantees in open environments?
- What are the theoretical limits of machine learning?
- Can general artificial intelligence be formally defined?
- How can we verify the safety and alignment of advanced AI systems?
- What is the minimum computational substrate required for intelligence?
- Can distributed systems be made fully fault tolerant under arbitrary failures?
- What are the limits of quantum computing algorithms?

---

# economics

- What mechanisms drive long-term economic growth across civilizations?
- How do technological shocks propagate through global economies?
- What models best predict systemic financial crises?
- Can economic inequality be modeled as an emergent property of network dynamics?
- What are the macroeconomic effects of widespread AI automation?
- How do institutions shape economic productivity over centuries?
- Can we design economic systems that are both efficient and equitable?

---

# materials-science

- Can we design room-temperature superconductors?
- What atomic structures produce extreme strength-to-weight ratios?
- Can materials self-heal at the molecular level?
- How can we engineer materials with programmable mechanical properties?
- What unknown phases of matter exist under extreme pressure or temperature?
- Can we design materials that efficiently convert heat directly to electricity?
- What limits the efficiency of photovoltaic materials?

# math

- Is the Riemann Hypothesis true?
- Do smooth solutions always exist for the Navier–Stokes equations?
- Are P and NP distinct complexity classes?
- What is the ultimate structure of prime numbers?
- Can a unified framework connect all major branches of mathematics?
- What are the limits of computability?
- Can new mathematical objects explain physical reality more fundamentally?

# medicine

- What biological processes drive aging at the cellular level?
- Can aging be slowed, halted, or reversed?
- What mechanisms initiate cancer in otherwise healthy tissue?
- How can immune systems be trained to eliminate cancer reliably?
- What causes neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's?
- Can personalized medicine predict optimal treatments for every individual?
- What biological markers predict disease decades before symptoms appear?

# philosophy

- What is consciousness and how does it arise from physical systems?
- Is consciousness substrate-independent?
- Do minds require biological organisms, or can artificial systems possess genuine awareness?
- What is the nature of subjective experience (qualia)?
- Can free will exist in a deterministic universe?
- What constitutes personal identity over time?
- What is the relationship between information and reality?
- Are mathematical truths discovered or invented?
- What is the nature of causality?
- Can morality be derived from objective principles?
- What obligations do humans have toward artificial intelligences?

# physics

- Can quantum mechanics and general relativity be unified?
- What is dark matter?
- What is dark energy?
- Why does the universe have the physical constants it does?
- Are there additional spatial dimensions?
- What is the true nature of spacetime?
- Does the multiverse exist?
- What happens inside black holes?
- How does quantum measurement work?
- Can energy be extracted from vacuum fluctuations?

# statistics

- What are the theoretical limits of causal inference from observational data?
- Can we develop universally reliable uncertainty quantification for machine learning models?
- How can we detect hidden bias in high-dimensional datasets?
- What statistical methods best handle extremely sparse or noisy data?
- Can we unify Bayesian and frequentist approaches in a deeper framework?
- How can statistical models remain interpretable while scaling to massive datasets?
- What are the limits of prediction in complex adaptive systems?
